A Three-Act Story

The Evolution of the Industry Model

1. The Age of IDMs

Early pioneers controlled the entire process in a vertically-integrated model, from design to manufacturing.

2. The Fabless-Foundry Rise

The value chain disaggregated, allowing "fabless" firms to outsource manufacturing to specialized "foundries".

3. The Era of Hybrid Models

With fab costs soaring to $40B+, even IDMs like Intel are adopting collaborative, hybrid strategies.

From total integration to deep specialization, the industry structure has profoundly transformed.
